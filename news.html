Development of Emergency Management System (EMS) for Mayo Hospital Lahore

November 2009

Mayo Hospital has been a prestigious institution of the Asian region since more than a century. Old cultural forces had made it quite difficult to automate the processes of the hospital.

Many previous attempts to implement IT systems at Mayo Hospital had failed. It was the first time ever in October 2009 that an installed Emergency Management System went live. Absorb was blessed with the honour of the development and implementation of this system. The EMS currently running is shortly planned to encompass all the business processes at the Mayo Emergency.

In the long run, all the other departments of the hospital are planned to be integrated in the system. The first one of them will most probably be the Pediatric Surgery Department.

Development of Complaint Management System for the Cantonment Board,Peshawar

November 2009

Absorb was entrusted with the development of a Complaint Management System for the Cantonment Board Peshawar in June 2009. The main aim of the government was to evolve an IT based, efficient and easy complaint redressal mechanism for the public.

The successfully developed CMS was deployed in the Peshawar Cantonment in October 2009. The system has got the capability of registering public complaints and tracking these department-wise and official-wise. The status of each complaint is available to the complainant on-line.

The system also aids the senior management to monitor the issues of the public visiting the Cantonment Office in addition to the real-time tracking of all the complaints.
